A comprehensive evaluation: The first visit takes approximately 1 and 1/2 to 2 hours and includes:

  • Reviewing the patient history, nutrution, previous clinic letters, laboratory test results, and the questionnaire you fill out in advance
  • A complete physical examination
  • Bioresonance testing[*] when appropriate
  • Further laboratory testing, if needed
  • Nutritional and dietary recommendations including supplements if recommended
  • A follow up plan

*Bioresonance testing is done with Bicom bioresonance device that is manufactured by Regumed GmbH in Germany to ISO 9001 quality standard. It complies with the European Union directive 93/42/EEC on medical devices.
